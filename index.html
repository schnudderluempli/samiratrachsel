(that‘s mostly when I take over)

besides my passion for collecting recipes, adapting them and endlessly turning them into new ones, I love to play around with food. why not play with food a little more? of course as long as it will be shared and appreciated. my admiration and curiosity for flavours, textures, colors and origins have a big influence on the work I do.

and the bachelor’s degree in design and trend research (that I’m currently doing), might also help with keeping up with the lastest trends in and around the food cosmos :)
